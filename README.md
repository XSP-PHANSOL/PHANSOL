## 🔥 Deflationary Strategy

PHANSOL is committed to a long-term deflationary path. Up to **12–15% of total supply (120M–150M $PHAN)** will be permanently burned through transparent, milestone-based mechanisms:

- **Milestone Burns** — Triggered at key growth milestones (holder counts, listings, community size targets)
- **Community-Voted Burns** — $PHAN holders vote via DAO governance (Phase 3)
- **Scheduled Burns** — Portion burned at the start of each roadmap phase

All burns are announced in advance, executed on-chain, and publicly verifiable. The 18% Ecosystem Reserve exists specifically to maintain price stability and enable responsible growth.
